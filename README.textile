h1. Sandycandy

A free app for kids to launch 'campaigns' to sell candy, prepare 'menus', take orders, quantities resquests, even ship candy orders and get paid online when possible.

dedicated to the 20 children who lost their young lives in the Sandy Hook shooting, Newton Ct, on Decemeber 14th 2012.
